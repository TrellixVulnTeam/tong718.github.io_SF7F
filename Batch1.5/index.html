About Us

Accounting Career Connect creates a solution to bridge the gap between graduates and employers, through mentorship to enhance the right attitude, skills, and knowledge needed on actual performance of job. It blends the experiences of today's business leader with tomorrow's- creating THE FUTURE TO BE NOW.
